# Included Papers

This file provides an overview of all papers included in the systematic review. Papers are organised into three tiers based on their relevance to the survey topic.

## Categorisation Scheme

| Category | Description | Typical Count |
|---|---|---|
| **Core** | Papers that directly propose or evaluate a novel method for few-view X-ray-to-CT reconstruction. | 20–40 |
| **Supporting** | Papers that contribute relevant techniques, architectures, loss functions, or training strategies used by core methods. | 15–30 |
| **Background** | Papers that provide foundational context, such as classical CT reconstruction theory, general deep learning surveys, or related medical imaging tasks. | 10–20 |

## Selection Process

Papers were identified through systematic database searches (see [search_strategy.md](../docs/search_strategy.md)) and screened against predefined inclusion/exclusion criteria (see [inclusion_exclusion_criteria.md](../docs/inclusion_exclusion_criteria.md)). The categorisation was performed collaboratively and reviewed for consistency.
